About KEAN

Bridging communities, fostering collaboration, and creating opportunities across borders

The KEAN platform is a collaborative initiative that brings together the cities of Kleve, Emmerich, Arnhem, and Nijmegen to promote cross-border cooperation, cultural exchange, and economic development in the region.

Our mission is to create a vibrant, interconnected community where residents, businesses, and organizations can work together to address common challenges and seize shared opportunities.
